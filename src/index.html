ABOUT_ME

May 19th 1994 born in Varna, Bulgaria. Early on, I developed a passion to create. Getting my first computer was the turning point for my creativity. I spent hundreds of hours in Paint and Microsoft Office until, Photoshop entered my life in a form of a DVD with a pirated version of CS3. Everything else stopped, Photoshop was my life for the majority of my teen years, creating countless designs ranging from fictional company logos to websites and branding projects. During university and later jobs, that creative spirit was dormant, up until the "Pandemic Restart". I am back - ready to improve and create.
